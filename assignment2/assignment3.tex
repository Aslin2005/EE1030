		then the value of $a$ is\\

 \vec{A}&=\frac{\vec{B}+\vec{C}}{2}\\ 
\implies \vec{A}&=\frac{\myvec{-6\\5}+\myvec{-2\\3}}{2}=\frac{\myvec{-8\\8}}{2}=\myvec{-4\\4}\\
\because \vec{A}&=\myvec{\frac{a}{3}\\4}\\
\implies a&=-4\times3\\ 
 a&=-12
